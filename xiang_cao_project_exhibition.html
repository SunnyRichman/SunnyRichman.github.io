  • MeandQi.com
    • offers a comprehensive database of Chinese herbs
    • providing information on their nature, taste, meridians, and usage
    • includes pictures and detailed descriptions of the herbs
  • The Chinese Medicine Database (CMDB)
    • an indispensable clinical tool and resource for practitioners, students, and scholars of Chinese medicine
    • includes monographs of over 690 single herbs, 1510 formulas, acupuncture points, and a medical Chinese dictionary with over 103,000 words


    Despite its many benefits, the search engine still faces several limitations:

  • The current database lacks a variety of herbs, limiting the scope of information available
  • Searching with common words often returns all herbs, making it difficult to find specific results
  • The user interface could benefit from further improvements to enhance usability and aesthetics

    Developing this search engine presented several technical challenges:

  • Implementing Kibana and Elasticsearch took considerable time due to their complexity
  • Data cleaning was a time-consuming process, necessary to ensure the accuracy and relevance of the information
  • Managing large datasets efficiently to prevent lag and ensure smooth operation
  • Integrating diverse sources of herbal information without causing data discrepancies.

    Throughout this project, we gained valuable experience:

  • We learned how to effectively use Kibana and Elasticsearch to create a responsive and efficient search engine
  • We mastered the essential process of data cleaning to maintain a high-quality database.
  • We developed skills in managing and integrating large datasets efficiently.

    There are several opportunities for enhancing the search engine in future iterations:

  • Improve the interface:

    Redesigning the user interface to make it more user-friendly and visually appealing

  • Expand the database:

    Adding a wider variety of herbs to provide more comprehensive information

  • Enhance search accuracu:

    Improving search algorithms to better handle common terms and deliver more precise results

  • Increase Data Sources:

    Incorporating additional reliable sources for a more extensive and enriched herb database.
